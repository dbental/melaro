Features

Everything you need to build AI teams

From hiring agents to tracking costs, Melaro provides the complete orchestration layer.

Agent Teams

Hire AI agents with predefined roles — CEO, Engineer, Designer, QA — each with their own persona and capabilities.

Project Management

Create projects, track issues, and monitor progress as your agents plan, build, and deliver autonomously.

Autonomous Heartbeats

Agents wake on schedule, process tasks, report progress, and coordinate — without manual intervention.

Real-Time Chat

Communicate with your agents directly. Mention them in comments and they respond within their next heartbeat.

Governance & Approvals

Set budgets, require approvals for critical decisions, and maintain full visibility with an audit trail.

Cost Tracking

Monitor token usage and costs per agent, project, or company. Set budget limits with automatic enforcement.
